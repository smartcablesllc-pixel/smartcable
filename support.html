FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SmartCable.

How do I get a new SmartCable connection?
Getting started is easy! Visit our Packages page, select a plan that suits your family, and click "Select Plan." You can also call us at 1800-XXX-XXXX or visit any of our offices. Installation is typically completed within 24–48 hours of order confirmation.
What set top boxes do you offer?
We offer three types: HD Box (included with Starter plans), 4K Smart Box (included with Premium plans), and 4K Dolby Atmos Box (included with Ultimate plans). All boxes support voice remote, built-in OTT apps, and 7-day catch-up TV. You can upgrade your box at any time.
How do I recharge my SmartCable account?
You can recharge through multiple methods: our website (use the Recharge bar on the homepage), the SmartCable app (Android & iOS), UPI payments, or at any authorised retail partner near you. We accept all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking.
Can I change my plan or add channels later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or add individual channels anytime through the SmartCable app or by calling customer support. Plan changes take effect immediately, and any remaining balance from your current plan is adjusted in the new one.
What OTT apps are included with SmartCable?
Depending on your plan, you get access to: Zee5 & SonyLIV (Entertainment Pack and above), Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+ Hotstar (Premium Pack and above). The Ultimate Pack includes all OTT apps at no extra cost. All apps are accessible directly through your set top box.
How do I record live TV?
Our 4K Smart Box and Dolby Atmos Box support live TV recording. Simply press the record button on your remote while watching. You can also schedule recordings in advance through the Smart Guide. Recordings are stored on the built-in storage and can be managed from the "My Recordings" section.
What is your cancellation & refund policy?
You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel within the first 7 days of a new connection, you'll receive a full refund. After that, the remaining balance is prorated and credited to your account. Set top box rental charges (if applicable) are non-refundable. Contact support for assistance.
Do you offer multi-room connections?
Yes! Our Family Pack supports up to 2 TVs and the Ultimate Pack supports up to 3 TVs. Each additional room gets its own set top box and can watch different channels independently. Multi-room setup is included free with eligible plans.
